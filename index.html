About the Home

Where tradition meets comfort

Welcome to Casa Banaras — a peaceful retreat tucked into the vibrant lanes of Godowlia, the beating heart of the oldest living city in the world.

Our spacious, airy rooms offer the perfect blend of comfort and tradition. Whether you're here to witness the Ganga Aarti, seek blessings at Kashi Vishwanath, or simply get lost in the spiritual energy of Banaras — this is your home away from home.

Ideal for families and groups of friends, with both Western and Indian toilet options, 24×7 hot water, fast Wi-Fi, and a location that puts everything within walking distance.

Your neighbourhood

Walk to the soul of the city

500 m · 6 min walk

Kashi Vishwanath Mandir

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, the spiritual epicenter of Banaras. Dawn darshan is unforgettable.

650 m · 8 min walk

Dashashwamedh Ghat

Watch the mesmerising Ganga Aarti every evening as priests offer fire, flowers and chants to the river.

700 m · 9 min walk

Manikarnika Ghat

The eternal ghat where time feels suspended — a profound window into the cycle of life.

300 m · 4 min walk

Kachori Gali & Paan

The legendary food lanes of Banaras — start your morning with kachori-sabzi and end with sweet paan.

11 km · 30 min drive

Sarnath

Where Buddha delivered his first sermon. The Dhamek Stupa and museum make for a peaceful half-day trip.

6 km · 20 min drive

BHU & Kashi Vishwanath (new)

The beautiful Banaras Hindu University campus and its golden-domed temple replica.
